About Us

"Market Leader in Retail Forex"

CK Markets is a multi-award winning global authority in the Forex industry and a broker your clients will trust. There is a reason why over 1.5 Million Clients Choose CK Markets for Forex Trading, Stock Indices Trading, Commondity Trading, Stock, Metals and Energies Trading.

Licensed and Regulated Broker

CK Markets is registered in St. Vincent & the Grenadine as an International Business Company with the registration number 24405 IBC 2017.

Globally Renowned

We have clients from over 196 countries and staff speaking over 30 languages. Our management has visited over 120 cities globally to understand clients’ and partners’ needs.

Focused on the Client

Size does not matter. At CK Markets the client comes first regardless of net capital worth, account type or size of investment. All our clients receive the same quality services, the same execution, and the same level of support. CK Markets was founded on these values, and that will not change.

Range of Trading Instruments

Our clients can choose to trade forex and CFDs on stock indices, commodities, stocks, metals and energies from the same trading account. With a wide range of trading instruments available from a single multi asset platform Ck Markets makes trading easier and efficient.

Transparent and Fair

At CK Markets what you see is what you get, with no hidden terms. Be that pricing, execution or promotions. What we advertise is what we give our clients, regardless of the size of their investment.

Easy and Convenient

All our systems are built and updated with the client in mind. Starting from our account opening procedure, to managing your account, depositing or withdrawing funds and finally trading, it’s all straightforward simple and easy to use for all our clients.

Pushing the boundaries

With many years of active trading experience across multiple platforms and markets, the progressive and dynamic CKs management team recognized that traditional web based forex brokers would be placed under increasing pressure due to their delays in executing trades, frequent system failures and poor overall customer service. As a result, CKs aims to lead the way forward through the use of cutting-edge technology, innovative systems solutions and unrivaled customer service.

By your side at every step

CKs clients are the priority, which is why everything we do is always with the goal of improving their overall trading experience. Our dedication to clients stems from our belief in offering a level of personal customer service that is second to none. The CKs customer support team is comprised of industry professionals who are available around the clock to help clients with anything they may need from initial registration to the withdrawal of trading profits.
